We Design Better.

Symmetry

This living space’s symmetrical design draws the eye immediately up to the wallpapered ceiling and ceiling fixture. The screens in the back of the room anchor the space, focusing attention on the unexpectedly tall piece of art over the fireplace.

Glamour

Sleek lines and exotic materials give this living room a glamorous look that sets it apart. The floor-to-ceiling bookcase has a metallic wall covering on the back to create a focal point. Although every piece of furniture is upholstered in a different plush fabric, they all sing the same song.

Coordination

The overstuffed chairs in this chic man cave match the two accent seats on the other side of the room for one uniform color combination. The all-white painted brick wall, full-length draperies and firewood are complementary textures rather than additional focal points.

Features

Like art, interior design speaks to each of us differently.

Contrast

Although the contemporary sleek black desk and lampshade stand out as the immediate focal draw in this bedroom, I love how the retro fur accent chairs contrast with them and the quirky zebra painted table.

Harmony

Soft color tones and subtle Shabby Chic style are more than welcoming in this guest bedroom.
